About Me

My name is Khamryn Clark. I began my coding journey in high school in 2014. There I learned the basics of HTML and CSS. From then on I didn't have a huge interest in coding and pursued a career in the medical field. After a few years in the field I realized that this was not for me. I've tried various jobs within the field and decided I needed a career switch. I went to explore options and looked over the skills that I have learned in the last few years and remembered that I have learned to code a while back. I looked into the field of software engineering and it sparked my motivation again.
I decided to take some free courses online and I was filled with passion and began loving to code. After the free tutorials and courses I signed up for a coding bootcamp at SheCodes. So far I have learned HTML, CSS, JavaScript, GitHub, Netlify, and Bootstrap. I have completed three projects so far. After I finish this coding bootcamp I will be learning more programming languages to help build my skills in front-end web development.
